Tucker Group

live website

Details

Tucker Group is a small business specializing in government contracts and acquisitions. The founder was interested in establishing a brand and needed a logo, deliverables, and a landing page for his prospective clients to visit. He wanted his brand to have a modern aerospace look. The fade animations were written using jQuery and the website features some jQuery plugins as well. It is fully responsive using vanilla JS and CSS (no WYSIWYG frameworks like Bootstrap) and includes a contact page powered by PHP.
